Maize & Blue Cupboard

What is the Maize & Blue Cupboard?

College students and staff are experiencing food insecurity at alarming rates. The Maize and Blue Cupboard is here to provide an immediate and comprehensive response for the U-M community. By offering resources, educational opportunities, compassionate support and more, they help students develop the skills to make informed decisions.

Who can access the Maize & Blue Cupboard?

Anyone with a valid U-M ID!

Where is the Maize & Blue Cupboard located?

Located in the basement of the Betsy Barbour Residence Hall, enter via the Maynard entrance only to ensure privacy for residents. Contact the MBC if you need ramp or elevator access.
Address: 420 S State St Ann Arbor, MI 48109
Phone: 734-936-2794
