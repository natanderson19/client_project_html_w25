UMSI Tutoring and Academic Support

UMSI Academic Success Team

The Academic Success Team at UMSI is dedicated to supporting students in every aspect of their academic journey. We connect students to a variety of tutoring and academic support resources aimed at enhancing learning and academic performance. Our comprehensive suite of resources includes:

  • UMSI Peer Tutoring: Focused support from fellow students who have excelled in their coursework and can provide insights and guidance on complex topics and assignments.
  • Programming Peer Tutoring: Personalized assistance in programming concepts and coding assignments, helping you build confidence and expertise in languages and tools critical to your coursework.
  • Math Peer Tutoring: Support in fundamental and advanced mathematical topics essential for success in data science and information analysis courses.
  • Additional Academic Support Resources: Including study groups, workshops, and seminars tailored to enhance academic skills and strategies.
  • Peer-to-Peer Tutor Directory: Access a network of qualified peer tutors available to provide personalized and flexible tutoring sessions.

UMSI Peer Tutoring Program

Our UMSI Peer Tutoring Program empowers students to achieve academic excellence through collaborative learning with peer tutors. Our tutors are selected based on their expertise in specific UMSI courses, providing support that is both targeted and relevant. Tutors help with comprehension of course material, preparation for exams, and development of effective study techniques.

This program is designed to foster a collaborative learning environment where students can share their challenges and breakthroughs in a supportive setting. For more information about how the Peer Tutoring Program can benefit you, or to inquire about becoming a tutor, please contact us at umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu.

To explore available peer tutors and find one that suits your needs, visit this page.

UMSI Math Peer Tutoring

Our Math Peer Tutoring service offers specialized support across a broad range of math topics critical for UMSI students. The subjects covered include:

  • Linear Algebra: Gain a better understanding of vector spaces, matrix operations, and their applications in computing and data science.
  • Statistics: Develop a robust foundation in statistical methods, concepts, and data analysis techniques essential for research and decision-making.
  • General Math Support: From calculus to quant analysis, get tailored help to ensure you succeed in all math-related areas of your academic journey.

Our tutors are equipped to break down complex problems and guide you through solution strategies, enabling you to grasp core concepts with confidence. Learn more about math peer tutors and schedule a session at this page.

Additional Resources

  • Sweetland Writing Center: Dedicated to improving student writing of all forms, offering personalized guidance and workshops that enhance writing proficiency and communication skills across disciplines.
  • English Language Institute: Provides tailored courses and resources for non-native English speakers, focusing on academic language proficiency and communication skills needed for successful participation in U-M’s vibrant academic community.
  • LS&A Math Lab: Access free tutoring services for a wide range of math courses, providing support designed to help students develop solid mathematical understanding and problem-solving skills.
  • Services for Students with Disabilities: Committed to accommodating students with disabilities by fostering an environment that is inclusive, equitable, and supportive of diverse learning needs.
