Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

What is CAPS?

CAPS is committed to creating an environment based on our values of multicultural, multi-disciplinary and multi-theoretical practices that allow our diverse student body to access care, receive high quality services and take positive pathways to mental health. They are happy to offer both in-person and virtual services.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides a range of clinical, preventative, and training services consistent with the practices and standards of a nationally accredited university counseling center. CAPS is committed to creating a safe and supportive environment.

What services are available to students?

CAPS covers a wide range of services for students. View this page to understand more about the scope of services CAPS provides. The following services are also available to students:

  • Mental Health Support Options
  • Sexualized Violence Resources
  • First Year Guide
  • Support for students impacted by campus climate concerns
